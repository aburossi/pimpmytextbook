// Bloom Levels

**1. Bloom Level: "Erinnern"**
   - Question Type: Recall-based.
   - Focus: Recognition and recall of facts.
   - Design: Straightforward questions requiring the learner to select the correct information.

**2. Bloom Level: "Verstehen"**
   - Question Type: Comprehension-based.
   - Focus: Explanation of ideas or concepts.
   - Design: Questions prompting learners to interpret or summarize {{content}} to demonstrate understanding.

---

// Question Types and Details

**1. Multiple Choice Questions**
- "type": "MultipleChoice"
- "bloom_level": "Erinnern" or "Verstehen".
- "question": Question text enclosed in <p> tags.
- "options": Array of answer choices.
  - Each choice includes:
    - "text": Text of the option.
    - "is_correct": Boolean (true or false).
    - "feedback": Explanation for correctness or incorrectness, with **bolded keywords** using <strong> tags.

**2. True/False Questions**
- "type": "TrueFalse"
- "bloom_level": "Erinnern" or "Verstehen".
- "question": Statement enclosed in <p> tags.
- "correct_answer": Boolean (true or false).
- Feedback:
  - "feedback_correct": Explanation for correct answers without bolded keywords.
  - "feedback_incorrect": Explanation for incorrect answers without bolded keywords.
